== Installation ==

**What To Do**

1. Download the zip to your hardrive.
2. Upload to your 'Plugin' Directory via On-site Uploader (zip).
3. Second Last, Click 'Activate Plugin'.
4. Have fun and don't blame me for hours spent playing!
